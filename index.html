Age of Empire 2 Resource Collector

Intruction

  • This is a small fan-made Age of Empire Theme picture guessing game. The goal of the game is to gather the exact amount of resources to upgrade.

  • There are 4 type of resource in AOE: Gold, Food, Stone and Wood correspond to the picture below. The player will have to guess the amount of resource each picture produce to match up the final value.

  • At every upgrade, the amount of resource that each picture generate and the total needed will be different. Each Loss will reset your progress. There are total of 4 ages in Age of Empire 2. Try to get to it! Good luck!
